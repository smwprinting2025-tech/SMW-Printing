About Us

We’re a group of students running a small 3D printing business at school. Our goal is to make affordable, fast 3D prints for classmates, clubs, and teachers—while learning real-world business and engineering skills.

Note: We take submitted 3D models and print them, but we do not design or model files ourselves.
