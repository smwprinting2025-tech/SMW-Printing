FAQ

Do we model?

No, we do not model YET, because not all of our team is trained to model.

Can I do multi-color prints?

Yes, but we quote the cost as it generates extra waste.

What files do we accept?

Links must be free on websites we get the files from.

Can I mix multiple material types together?

No, unless it is for support, which costs slightly more for better print quality.
